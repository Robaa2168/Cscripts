Tropicana renewables project on track

Wind turbine foundations have been completed and we have begun piling for the solar farm. The next milestone will be the arrival of the turbine components, due by May.

The renewables project is expected to reduce Tropicana’s diesel and gas consumption for power generation by 96% and 50% respectively; cutting carbon emissions by approximately 65,000 tonnes annually over the life of the agreement.

We pursue value-creating opportunities involving other minerals, where we can leverage our existing assets, shareholdings, skills and experience.
